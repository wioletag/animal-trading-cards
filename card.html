Sea Lion

Sea lions balance a ball utilizing their whiskers and not their nose.

  • Scientific Name: Otariinae
  • Average Weight: 300-1,000kg (660-2,200lbs)
  • Average Lifespan: 15-22 years
  • Habitat: Coastal waters and rocky shorelines

Sea lions are sea mammals characterized by external ear flaps, long foreflippers, the ability to walk on all fours, and short, thick hair.There are seven species of Sea Lions in the world. They all belong to a group of animals known as pinnipeds.
